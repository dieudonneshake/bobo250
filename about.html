Our Story

Bringing premium electronics to Musanze since 2020

Who We Are

BOBO250 Electronics Shop is your premier destination for quality electronics in Rwanda's Musanze District, located at the foot of Mount Karisimbi. Founded by passionate tech enthusiasts, we've grown from a small shop to the region's most trusted electronics retailer.

Our Vision

To empower Rwanda's digital transformation by providing access to cutting-edge technology at affordable prices. We believe everyone deserves quality electronics that enhance their daily lives and business operations.
